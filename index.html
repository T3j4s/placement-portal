About TPO:

Training and Placement Cell facilitates on-campus placement opportunities and internship prospects and conducts comprehensive pre-placement training for students.
The Cell provides soft skill training to all pre-final year students to enable them to get acquainted with the current requirements of the corporate world and to excel in it. Students are coached to participate effectively in interviews and group discussions and to be successfully recruited. Additionally, training is given to pre-final year students for answering aptitude tests (both online and offline tests) and to face interviews and group discussions. The Cell organizes talks by HR personnel of various companies to acquaint students with the expectations of the recruiting companies and the various stages of the placement process.Internship opportunities in various reputed companies have also been provided for students during the vacation period.
Career counselling for pursuing higher studies in India and abroad is provided to the students along with seminars and expert talks for GATE, SAT, MCAT, LSAT, GMAT, GRE, IELTS and the TOEFL. The Cell conducts entrepreneurship development programs to motivate aspiring students through various workshops, seminars & expert talks.
The Institute aims at providing organizations with engineers who are an appropriate fit for the organization's needs.There are several activities to keep students busy, in their various areas of interest.
